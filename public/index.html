My Trajectory

A brief timeline of what I have been doing so far. (I hope to get into more details of relevant projects as I further develop this page)

  • July 2009

    Mechanical Engineering 101

    Started a Mechanical Engineering course at UDESC (Universidade do Estado de Santa Catarina). I will save you of the story of the kid that liked to take thing apart, all engineers tell this one.

  • March 2010

    Velociraptor Baja-SAE

    I began participating in the Baja-SAE Velociraptor team. The focus of the project is to design and build an off-road vehicle for competitions between universities with other students. Good place to learn a lot about engineering and team-work.

  • December 2010

    Summer Work/Travel Exchange

    Since I was a kid I always enjoyed the old video-games, my older brother gave me a dictionary to understand what I had to do in the games and with that I learned how to write and read in English. Going abroad was a fast way to finally learn how to speak. I spent three months working in a restaurant at Nascar Sports Grille in Orlando/Florida

  • September 2011

    Own Baja Project

    After learning a lot in the Baja team, I’ve decided to design and build my own. Together with my older brother (also Mec. Engineer) we did just that. We designed it from scratch and build in our garage, we took almost a year and a half to finish (only weekends). Several years have passed and our mom is still mad at us. (Hope to go into more depth of the process here later on)

  • December 2013

    Thesis, Particle Swarm Truss Optimization

    Time to work on my thesis! I fell in love with optimization the moment that the professor started explaning it to me, I had a lot of fun working on this and also learned a lot about optimization and Finite Elements Method.

  • February 2014

    Internship, WEG - Wind Power

    I was lucky enough to get a position in the brand new Wind Power department at WEG. Wind Turbines are an awesome product to learn and work with. I got a position in Supply Chain and did a little bit of everything in there as an intern.

  • July 2014

    Graduation and Job!

    Finally I graduated! In the same month I got an effective position at WEG, still working with Wind Turbines but now more focused on Developing Suppliers for components of a new Wind Turbine (the AGW3.3).

  • August 2015

    Began my Master's Degree

    Began a Masters course in Project, Analysis and Optimization of Mechanical Systems. Never thought that I would miss college. I am hoping to use this knowledge in a more technical position later on.

  • September 2015

    Nemo Project

    Now I am together with two other crazy engineers to design and build a Hydrofoil Bycicle. We hope to get that done in a year, I will try to record part of the process in here.
